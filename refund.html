Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled for tournament entry fees and related payments made through CSIL Tournaments.

1. General Rule

Tournament entry fees are generally non-refundable after successful registration, because a reserved slot may block participation for other players or teams.

2. Refunds May Be Considered In These Cases

  • The tournament is canceled by the organizers
  • The payment was charged more than once by mistake
  • A technical issue caused a confirmed payment without valid registration
  • The event is materially changed and participation becomes impossible

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • You decide not to participate after registration is completed
  • You are disqualified for cheating, rule violations, or misconduct
  • You missed the tournament start time or failed to appear
  • Your team withdraws voluntarily after confirmation

4. Refund Request Deadline

Refund requests must be submitted within a reasonable time after the issue occurs. We recommend contacting support within 7 days.

5. Processing Time

If a refund is approved, processing time may vary depending on the payment provider, bank, or card issuer.
