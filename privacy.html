Privacy Policy

We may collect the following information:

We use your information to:

Payments may be processed through third-party payment providers. We do not store full bank card details on our servers unless explicitly stated by the payment provider.

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information only with trusted third-party services needed to operate the platform, such as payment services, email services, SMS verification providers, and hosting providers.

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. We will review and respond within a reasonable period.

8. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies, local storage, and analytics tools to improve website functionality, remember user preferences, and better understand platform usage.

9. Children’s Privacy
